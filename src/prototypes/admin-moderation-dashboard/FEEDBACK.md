## Current Prototype Snapshot

The prototype is a mobile-first Personal Dashboard direction for administrators. It keeps the dashboard as the stable venue and adds admin-specific backlog cards.

Top-level cards currently show current actionability, not volume-over-time:

- Pages for protection: `17 unresolved`, `6 no admin reply`, `22h longest wait`
- Pages for speedy deletion: `4 awaiting review`, `2 need decision`, `3h longest wait`

Drill-in views currently exist for:

- Pages for protection
- Pages for speedy deletion

The main design bet is that the dashboard should help admins answer: "where is my judgment needed right now?"

## How We Will Process Feedback

For each feedback item, separate:

- Observation: what the reviewer noticed or reacted to.
- Underlying concern: what problem may be behind the feedback.
- Design assumption affected: what belief in the prototype this challenges.
- Possible response: keep, change, remove, test, or defer.
- Decision: what we actually choose after discussion.
